Landmark RCT Cardiology

PARADIGM-HF Trial

McMurray et al. • NEJM 2014 • N=8,442
Bottom line: Sacubitril/valsartan outperformed enalapril and reset first-line HFrEF therapy.

Clinical Question

Does sacubitril/valsartan reduce cardiovascular death and heart failure hospitalization compared with enalapril in HFrEF?

Study Design

Multicenter randomized controlled trial in symptomatic HFrEF, with 8,442 participants and reduced LVEF eligibility criteria.

Key Findings

The primary composite endpoint was reduced by about 20%, and the trial was stopped early for benefit.

Practice Impact

ARNI therapy displaced ACE inhibitor monotherapy as a foundational component of guideline-directed therapy in HFrEF.

Cardiology Core Topic

Acute Decompensated Heart Failure

Last reviewed: March 10, 2026

Overview

+
Clinical frame

ADHF is a rapid or gradual worsening of heart failure symptoms requiring urgent therapy or hospitalization.

Presentation

+
Typical features

Dyspnea, orthopnea, edema, elevated JVP, pulmonary edema, fatigue, and weight gain are common bedside clues.

Diagnosis

+
Workup

Clinical congestion plus BNP or NT-proBNP, chest imaging, ECG, troponin, renal function, echocardiography, and search for precipitant.

Management

+
Immediate priorities

IV loop diuretics, oxygen as needed, vasodilators when appropriate, treatment of precipitant, and early optimization of GDMT before discharge.
